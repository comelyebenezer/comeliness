LogiTrack Supply Chain

Client

LogiTrack Global

Timeline

14 Months

Role

Full Platform Build

Scale

10K+ Shipments/Day

The Challenge

LogiTrack operated across 15 countries with disconnected systems — each region used different tracking software, spreadsheets, or manual processes. This created blind spots in the supply chain, delayed shipments, and made it impossible to provide customers with accurate ETAs. The company was losing $2M annually due to inefficiencies.

Our Approach

We built a unified platform with real-time GPS tracking, automated route optimization, and predictive analytics. The system integrates with IoT sensors on vehicles and warehouses, providing end-to-end visibility. We used Vue.js for the dashboard, Python for the analytics engine, and Redis for real-time data processing.

Key Features

Features include live map tracking with geofencing, automated carrier selection, warehouse inventory management, customer-facing tracking portal, predictive delay alerts, and comprehensive analytics dashboards with custom report generation.

The Result

LogiTrack achieved full supply chain visibility across all 15 countries within 3 months of deployment. Shipment delays dropped by 60%, customer satisfaction scores improved by 45%, and the company expanded to 3 new markets using the platform's scalable infrastructure.
