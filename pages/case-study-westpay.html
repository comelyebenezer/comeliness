The Challenge

West Africa's underbanked population needed accessible financial services. Traditional banking infrastructure couldn't reach rural communities, and existing mobile money solutions lacked advanced features like investment tracking, budgeting tools, and international transfers. The client needed a secure, scalable platform that works on low-bandwidth connections.

Our Approach

We built a cross-platform mobile app using Flutter for consistent iOS and Android experience. The backend uses a microservices architecture on Firebase with real-time sync capabilities. We implemented biometric authentication, end-to-end encryption, and an offline-first architecture that works in areas with poor connectivity.

Key Features

The app includes peer-to-peer transfers, bill payments, savings goals with automated contributions, investment portfolio tracking, international money transfers, merchant payments via QR codes, and a financial literacy module with personalized insights.

The Result

WestPay became the fastest-growing fintech app in West Africa within 6 months of launch. Transaction volume grew 300% quarter-over-quarter. The platform now processes over $50M in monthly transactions and has partnerships with 3 major telecommunications providers for carrier billing integration.
